# Essay Skeleton · 文稿推进骨架

The advance order for distilling a chosen synthesis scope — one file, several, an
n-day window, or a theme — into a public-account essay. The skeleton is
field-independent; the path names are fixed for vd-research.
本文件给出"把指定的综合研判范围——单份、多份、某个 n 天窗口或某个主题——提炼为
公众号文稿"的推进顺序。骨架与领域无关；路径名固定为 vd-research 约定。

---

## Order of advance | 推进顺序（默认骨架）

1. **Open on the insight, not on the reports** — lead with one falsifiable thesis
   stated as the firm's own analysis. The reports are **cited as evidence**, never
   narrated as the subject: no "我们读了 N 份研报", no "这些研报的共同主线", no "把
   它们摞在一起". State what is true and why it matters; let the citations carry the
   provenance. The thesis is one or two sentences a reader can repeat, and future
   data could refute.
   **开篇即洞见，而非研报本身**——以一条可证伪的主张领起，并作为本机构自己的分析陈述。
   研报是**被引用的证据**，绝不作为叙述主体：不写"我们读了 N 份研报"、"这些研报的共同
   主线"、"把它们摞在一起"。陈述什么为真、为何重要；让引用承载出处。主张是一两句能被
   复述、且能被未来数据推翻的话。
   - ✓ *"这一轮 AI 真正在重新分布的不是产能，而是分配权：掌握分配权的一端攫取价值，
     没有议价权的一端承担成本。"*（直接立洞见）
   - ✗ *"六月这一个月，我们读了六十多份研报……它们浮出同一条主线。"*（复述研报来源）

2. **Scope the input — the reader's call** — the user names what to distill: one
   `synthesis/*.md`, several, an n-day rolling window, or a theme sliced across
   files. Name every source drawn from and each one's `generated_at`; the essay is
   a projection of those files, nothing more. Add no number or claim the sources
   do not carry; a figure flagged *single-source* or *待核* is not hardened. A
   wider scope forces a tighter claim, never a longer list.
   **范围由读者定**——用户指定提炼什么：一份 `synthesis/*.md`、多份、某个 n 天滚动
   窗口，或跨文件的某个主题。点名所据每一份来源及其 `generated_at`；文稿是这些文件的
   投影，仅此而已。不新增源文未承载的数字或主张；标过"单一信源/待核"的不得坐实。范围
   越宽，主张越收窄，绝不让清单越长。

3. **Advance by cause, and name the conflicts** — each movement inherits the last
   one's conclusion and answers the question it raised (*why → so what → so who
   wins, who pays*), and along the way it surfaces the real contradictions,
   cross-points, and audit-points from the synthesis, each with its evidence and a
   verdict. A conflict named without evidence and resolution is gossip; with both,
   it is analysis. Refuse "第一、第二、第三" enumeration and copied tables — this is
   prose more coherent than the audit, not the audit reprinted.
   **因果推进，并点名矛盾**——每段承接上段结论、回答它抛出的问题（*为什么 → 于是怎样 →
   谁赢、谁埋单*），并沿途把综合研判里真实的矛盾、交叉点、审计点摆出来，各带证据与裁决。
   只点名、不给证据与裁决，是八卦；两者都给，才是分析。拒绝"一二三"罗列与照搬表格——
   这是比审计更连贯的散文，不是审计的复印件。

4. **Stand on a binary spine** — choose one contrast that runs the whole essay
   (upstream vs downstream, raise vs absorb, structural vs cyclical), so the lay
   reader never loses the thread.
   **立在二分脊柱上**——选一个贯穿全文的对照（上游 vs 下游、涨价 vs 消化、结构 vs
   周期），让外行始终不丢线。

5. **Cite numbers — real basis, cross-validated, not a wall** — every load-bearing
   claim carries its figure **and** its source (which report, which date, the
   number — e.g. *"Bernstein 6/23：商品内存自 25Q3 涨约 4.5 倍"*). The number serves
   the causal argument, never replaces it, never piles into a list. **Before
   writing, cross-validate each load-bearing figure against a live source (web)** —
   the synthesis is the spine, the web is the check. State only what survives;
   correct what conflicts (e.g. an OEM *system* price hike of ~15–20% wrongly read
   as ">50%", which is a *component/spot* figure); drop what nothing corroborates.
   A figure flagged *single-source* or *待核* is not hardened; never invent a number.
   This is the anti-hallucination gate.
   **列数字——带真实依据、经交叉验证、不堆成墙**——每个承重主张都带数字**与**出处
   （哪份报告、哪个日期、那个数——如 *"Bernstein 6/23：商品内存自 25Q3 涨约 4.5 倍"*）。
   数字服务于因果论证，不替代它、不堆成清单。**落笔前，把每个承重数字对照在线来源
   （联网）交叉验证**——综合研判是脊柱，联网是校验。只写经得起验证的；冲突的要更正
   （例如把 OEM *整机* 提价约 15–20% 误读成 ">50%"，后者是*组件/现货*口径）；无任何
   印证的删去。标过"单一信源/待核"的不得坐实；绝不杜撰。这是防幻觉闸门。

6. **State and defend the viewpoint** — make the stance explicit and back each
   judgment with its plain reason. An essay is allowed to conclude; what it is not
   allowed to do is conclude without a "because".
   **给出并支撑观点**——立场写明，每个判断都附白话理由。文章可以下结论；不可以的是
   下了结论却没有"因为"。

7. **Open the audit** — before closing, say plainly what would change the view
   and what to watch next (one short paragraph, no table). Candor is the source of
   credibility; a public essay that cannot be wrong is advertising.
   **敞开审计**——收束前，用白话写明"什么会改变这个判断、接下来盯什么"（一小段，
   不用表）。坦白是可信度的来源；一篇不可能错的公众号文是广告。

8. **Aphoristic coda + front matter** — close on one contrastive sentence, not a
   recap. Front matter carries `source`, `generated_at`, `window`/`date_range`,
   and a one-line *非投资建议*.
   **格言式收束 + front matter**——以一句对照句收尾，不复述。front matter 记 `source`、
   `generated_at`、`window`/`date_range` 与一句*非投资建议*。

## Front matter | 文稿元数据

```yaml
---
document: vd-research — Public Essay
scope: single | window | multi | theme     # which mode produced this · 产出模式
sources:                                    # one or more · 一或多
  - synthesis/rolling-window-YYYYMMDD-YYYYMMDD.md (generated_at <copied>)
generated_at: <real UTC at write time>
window: YYYYMMDD — YYYYMMDD                  # if scope is window/multi · 窗口模式时填
language: zh-CN
note: 非投资建议
---
```

Output path: `public-essays/YYYYMMDD_<short-topic>.md` — date is the essay's
write date; topic is a short hyphenated slug.
输出路径：`public-essays/YYYYMMDD_<short-topic>.md`——日期为成稿日，topic 为短连字符 slug。

## Self-check | 自检清单

- [ ] Does it open on the insight (the firm's analysis), with reports cited as evidence — not narrated ("我们读了 N 份研报 / 同一条主线")? |
      是否开篇即洞见（本机构分析），研报作引用证据——而非复述（"我们读了 N 份研报/同一条主线"）？
- [ ] Is every institution cited by one canonical English name (no 花旗/Citi or 高盛/GS mixing), and is the in-house source (K-Research) anonymized as 第三方研报? |
      每家机构是否只用一个英文规范名（无花旗/Citi、高盛/GS 混用），自有研报（K-Research）是否以"第三方研报"匿名？
- [ ] Was each load-bearing figure cross-validated against a live source, conflicts corrected, uncorroborated numbers dropped? |
      每个承重数字是否对照在线来源交叉验证、冲突已更正、无印证者已删？
- [ ] Can the opening claim be repeated in one sentence, and could future data refute it? |
      开篇主张能否一句复述、且能被未来数据推翻？
- [ ] Is every source drawn from named, each with its `generated_at`, and the scope mode recorded? |
      所据每一份来源是否都点名并附 `generated_at`，且记录了范围模式？
- [ ] Does the body advance by cause and form one story, not a reprinted audit table? |
      正文是否因果推进、成一个故事，而非审计表的复印？
- [ ] Are the major conflicts / cross-points / audit-points named, each with evidence and a verdict? |
      主要矛盾/交叉点/审计点是否都点名，并各带证据与裁决？
- [ ] Is there one binary spine running the whole essay? |
      是否有一根二分脊柱贯穿全文？
- [ ] Does every load-bearing number carry its real basis (report + date + figure), with no number-wall? |
      每个承重数字是否带真实依据（报告+日期+数），且无数字墙？
- [ ] Is the viewpoint explicit, and does every judgment carry a "because"? |
      观点是否明确、每个判断是否都带"因为"？
- [ ] Is there a plain-prose "what would change my mind / what to watch"? |
      是否有白话的"什么会改变判断/盯什么"？
- [ ] Does it close on one contrastive line, with front matter carrying source + real `generated_at` + 非投资建议? |
      是否以一句对照句收尾，front matter 含来源 + 真实 `generated_at` + 非投资建议？
