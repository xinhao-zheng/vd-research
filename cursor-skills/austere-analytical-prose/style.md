# Language: Syntax, Diction, Rhetoric · 语言风格：句法、用词、修辞

The rules of execution for the austere analytical voice. Most carry an example
(✓) and a counter-example (✗).
本文件给出"冷峻思辨学术体"的落笔规则。多数规则配范例（✓）与反例（✗）。

---

## Diction | 用词

1. **Zero filler.** Cut every "develops at high speed / increasingly severe / of
   great importance / strong support / far-reaching impact / not to be ignored".
   **零套话**。删尽"飞速发展、日益严峻、十分重要、强有力的支撑、深远影响、不容
   忽视"等填充语。
   - ✗ *As X develops at high speed, it is of great importance...*
     随着……的飞速发展，具有十分重要的意义
   - ✓ State directly what is missing and what follows.
     直接陈述缺了什么、于是会怎样

2. **Every adjective/adverb must be load-bearing.** Do not lift mood with
   adjectives; emphasis comes from meaning, not from tone.
   **每个形容词／副词必须承重**。不用形容词拔高情绪；强调靠语义，不靠语气。
   - ✗ extremely important, highly critical, enormous potential
     极其重要、非常关键、巨大潜力
   - ✓ Replace "important" with its consequence: *if it is missing, everything
     downstream must absorb the risk it leaves.*
     把"重要"换成它的后果："若缺它，下游的一切都得承担它留下的风险。"

3. **Carry concepts on concrete verbs**: bind, sever, anchor, collapse, expose,
   exhaust, displace, bear (weight), close (a gap) — not "carry out / realize /
   do the work of".
   **用具象动词承载概念**：绑定、切断、锚定、坍缩、暴露、耗尽、移置、承载、弥合
   ——而非"进行／实现／做好……工作"。

4. **Define a concept precisely on first use**, placing the English or a formal
   definition in parentheses where needed.
   **概念首次出现即给精确定义**，必要时把英文或形式定义置于括号内。

---

## Syntax & rhythm | 句法与节奏

5. **The em-dash inserts qualification and reversal** — the most visible rhythm
   marker of this voice, used for supplement, apposition, and sharp turn.
   **破折号插入限定与转折**——这是本风格最显著的节奏标志，用于补充、同位、急转。
   - ✓ *"The gap is structural, not a matter of degree — adding resources
     improves the result but cannot supply what it structurally lacks."*
     "这一缺口是结构性的，而非程度问题——增加投入能改善结果，却无法补上它结构上欠缺的东西。"

6. **After a long clause, drop one very short sentence** to land the
   conclusion and create stress.
   **长句铺陈后，用一个极短句砸下结论**，制造重音。
   - ✓ *"The method scales. The judgment does not."*
     "方法可以扩展。判断不能。"

7. **The colon carries "expand / define / consequence"**: A: B. Let the colon
   replace "that is" and "in other words".
   **冒号承担"展开／定义／后果"功能**：A：B。让冒号替代"也就是说""即"。

8. **Parallelism serves contrast only**, never momentum. Use parallel structure
   to throw one binary into relief, not to stack synonyms.
   **排比只为对照服务**，不为气势。用平行结构凸显一个二分，而非堆叠同义。
   - ✓ *"Strict where the rule binds; open where it falls silent."*
     "规则约束处从严；规则沉默处放手。"
   - ✗ three or four near-synonym clauses in a row, for force
     用三四个近义分句排比来加强语气

## Stance | 姿态

9. **"我们" for the act, "本文" for the document; never "笔者" or "文章".** Use
   "we" (我们) for what the authors do — argue, show, give, release; use "this
   paper" (本文) to frame the document — what it does, contributes, declines to
   do. Both state method and claim, never sentiment.
   **"我们"述行动，"本文"指文章；不用"笔者""文章"。** "我们"用于作者的动作——论证、
   给出、公开；"本文"用于框定文章本身——做了什么、贡献什么、不做什么。二者只陈述
   方法与主张，不用于抒情。

10. **Honesty over rhetoric.** Better to write "this is an open problem", "this
    is the least settled point" than to paper over a weakness. The coldness
    comes from candor, not from force.
    **诚实压过修辞**：宁可写"这是开放问题""此处最未定"，也不掩盖弱点。冷峻来自
    坦白，不是来自强硬。

## One-pass clean | 一键清洗流程（润色旧文时）

1. Delete every filler phrase and mood-adjective. | 删除所有套话与情绪形容词。
2. Swap abstract noun-phrases for concrete verbs. | 把抽象名词短语换成具象动词。
3. Find, for each long paragraph, a short sentence to land as its stress. |
   给每个长段找一个可作"重音"的短句收尾。
4. Check that em-dashes / colons serve rhythm, not random punctuation. |
   检查破折号／冒号是否用于节奏，而非随意点缀。
5. Reread: is there any sentence that carries no new information? Delete it. |
   通读：是否还有一句话不承载新信息？删掉。
