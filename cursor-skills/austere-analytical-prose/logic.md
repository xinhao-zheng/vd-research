# Argument Skeleton · 论证推进骨架

The advance order of the austere analytical voice. The illustrations are
domain-neutral; the skeleton is field-independent — it holds for a proof, an
empirical study, or a position piece alike.
本文件给出"冷峻思辨学术体"的推进顺序。范例均为领域中性；骨架与具体领域
无关——无论理论证明、实证研究还是立场论文皆适用。

---

## Order of advance | 推进顺序（默认骨架）

1. **Narrow the claim** — open by compressing the thesis to one falsifiable
   core, and announce the boundary of the whole piece. It does not seek to be
   large; it seeks to be hard.
   **收窄主张**——开篇把论点压到一句可证伪的核，并预告全文边界。它不求大，求硬。
   - e.g. *"The claim is deliberately narrow, and falsifiable in form."*
     范例："本文的主张刻意收窄，且在形式上可证伪。"

2. **Gap first** — rewrite "the problem" as a structural absence, and argue why
   it will not vanish as scale or degree improves.
   **缺口先行**——把"问题"重写为"一处结构性的缺失"，并论证它为何不会随规模或程度
   改善而消失。
   - e.g. *"The gap is structural, not a matter of degree — adding resources
     improves the result but cannot supply what it structurally lacks."*
     范例："这一缺口是结构性的，而非程度问题——增加投入能改善结果，却无法补上它
     结构上欠缺的东西。"

3. **Advance by cause, refuse enumeration** — each paragraph inherits the prior
   one's conclusion; join with "because / therefore / otherwise", not "first,
   second, third".
   **因果推进，拒绝罗列**——每个段落承接上一段的结论；用"因为／于是／否则"连接，
   而非"第一、第二、第三"。
   - Anti-pattern (drop it): list the problems 1-2-3, then list the remedies
     1-2-3, two columns in parallel with no causal link.
     反模式（弃用）：把问题列成一二三，再把对策列成一二三，两列平行而无因果。

4. **A binary spine** — stand the argument on one clear contrast (means vs end,
   scarce vs abundant, signal vs noise) so a single axis runs through the whole
   text.
   **二分脊柱**——把论证立在一个清晰的对照上（如 手段 vs 目的、稀缺 vs 充裕、
   信号 vs 噪声），让全文有一根贯穿到底的脊柱。

5. **Proposition-shape it** — write the hardest insight as a named, checkable
   proposition, then derive a corollary from it.
   **命题化**——把最硬的洞见写成可命名、可检验的命题，再由命题推出推论。
   - e.g. *"Proposition: a system's reliability is bounded by its least reliable
     component; improving the others leaves the bound unchanged."*
     范例："命题：系统可靠性以其最弱一环为上界；改善其余部分并不移动该上界。"

6. **Evidence by contrast** — use one table to map an abstract requirement to a
   concrete mechanism or datum (requirement ↔ mechanism, claim ↔ datum), so the
   claim is checkable rather than left hanging.
   **证据对照**——用一张表把抽象要求映射到具体机制／数据（要求 ↔ 机制、主张 ↔
   数据），让主张可被逐条检读，而非空悬。

7. **Falsifiability + limitations section** — devote one section to "what
   evidence would refute the thesis" and "what this work does not do". This is
   the source of credibility; do not omit it.
   **可证伪 + 局限专节**——单设一节，列出"何种证据将推翻本论题"以及"本研究未做
   什么"。这是可信度的来源，不可省。
   - e.g. *"Four observations would disconfirm this argument..."*
     范例："四项观察将证伪这一论述……"

8. **Aphoristic coda** — close by compressing the whole into one or two
   contrastive sentences — no summary, no outlook, no slogan.
   **格言式收束**——结语把全篇压成一两句对照句——不总结、不展望、不喊口号。
   - e.g. *"What is automated grows cheap; what must be chosen stays dear."* /
     *"The method scales. The judgment does not."*
     范例："被自动化的变廉价；须被抉择的仍昂贵。" ／ "方法可以扩展。判断不能。"

## Self-check | 自检清单

- [ ] Can the claim be stated in one sentence, and can you imagine a
      counter-example? | 主张能否用一句话说清，且能想象出反例？
- [ ] Is "the problem" written as a structural gap, not as "very important"? |
      "问题"是否被写成结构性缺口，而非"很重要"？
- [ ] Are paragraphs joined by cause, or laid out in parallel? (the latter must
      be rewritten) | 段落之间是因果，还是平行罗列？（后者要改）
- [ ] Is there one binary spine running through the whole text? |
      是否有一根二分脊柱贯穿全文？
- [ ] Is the core insight distilled into a proposition? |
      核心洞见是否提炼成了命题？
- [ ] Is at least one abstract claim mapped to concrete evidence in a table? |
      是否至少有一处抽象主张被映射到表格中的具体证据？
- [ ] Is there a "falsifiability / limitations" section? |
      是否有"可证伪／局限"专节？
- [ ] Is the ending an aphoristic contrast, not a slogan-like outlook? |
      结尾是格言式对照，还是口号式展望？（后者要改）
