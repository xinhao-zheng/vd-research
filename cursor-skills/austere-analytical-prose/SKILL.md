# Austere Analytical Prose · 冷峻思辨学术体

## What this is | 这是什么

In one line: **a voice built on one narrow, falsifiable claim, advanced by
causal chains rather than enumeration, written in spare, load-bearing language,
its rhythm cut by em-dashes and short sentences, and closed by an aphoristic
contrast.**
一句话：**以一条收窄、可证伪的主张为核心，用因果链而非罗列推进论证，语言克制、
精确、每词承重，靠破折号与短句切分节奏，并以格言式对照句收束的文体。**

Its opposite is the bloated "report register": stance first, filler phrases,
problems and remedies listed in parallel, adjectives doing the lifting. This
style stays deliberately far from that.
它的反面是堆砌套话的"报告体／学报腔"：立场先行、套话填充、把问题与对策平行罗列、
用形容词拔高。本风格刻意远离后者。

## Style fingerprint | 风格指纹（八项，与 logic.md 八步一一对应）

1. **Narrow, falsifiable claim** — open by compressing the thesis to its
   smallest, hardest core, and state what would refute it.
   **主张收窄且可证伪**——开篇即把论点压到最小、最硬的核，并声明"什么会推翻它"。
2. **Gap first** — do not say "important"; name precisely what is missing and
   why it is structural, not a matter of degree.
   **缺口先行**——不说"很重要"，而精确指出"缺了什么、为何这是结构性而非程度问题"。
3. **Causal advance** — every step is "because A, therefore B"; refuse parallel
   enumeration.
   **因果推进**——每一步是"因为 A 所以 B"，环环相扣；拒绝平行罗列。
4. **Binary spine** — stand the whole argument on one clear contrast (means vs
   end, scarce vs abundant, signal vs noise) so a single axis runs through it.
   **二分脊柱**——把全文立在一个清晰对照上（手段 vs 目的、稀缺 vs 充裕、信号 vs
   噪声），让单一轴线贯穿始终。
5. **Proposition** — distill the core insight into a named, checkable
   proposition, then derive its corollary.
   **命题化**——把核心洞见提炼成可命名、可检验的命题，再由命题推出推论。
6. **Evidence table** — map an abstract claim to concrete mechanism or data in
   a single table, so it can be read line by line rather than taken on faith.
   **证据对照表**——用一张表把抽象主张映射到具体机制或数据，使其可被逐条检读。
7. **Honest open-problems / limitations section** — mark the unresolved, and
   write plainly what evidence would lose the bet.
   **诚实的开放问题／局限专节**——主动划出未解部分，写明"何种证据将判输"。
8. **Aphoristic coda** — do not summarize or exhort; compress the whole into one
   or two sharp contrastive sentences.
   **格言式收束**——不总结、不喊口号，把全篇压成一两句锋利的对照句。

## How to use | 怎么用

**Writing new** — first read [logic.md](logic.md) to build the argument skeleton
(claim → gap → causal chain → binary spine → proposition → evidence →
falsifiability → coda), then write per [style.md](style.md).
**写新文**——先读 [logic.md](logic.md) 搭论证骨架（主张 → 缺口 → 因果 → 脊柱 →
命题 → 证据 → 可证伪 → 收束），再依 [style.md](style.md) 落笔。

**Polishing existing** — first clean the language sentence by sentence per
[style.md](style.md) (cut filler, swap in load-bearing verbs, fix rhythm), then
check the argument chain per [logic.md](logic.md) for a missing
"falsifiability" and "limitations".
**润色旧文**——先按 [style.md](style.md) 逐句清洗语言（删套话、换承重动词、调节奏），
再按 [logic.md](logic.md) 检查论证链是否成立、是否缺"可证伪"与"局限"。

## One contrast | 一个最小对照

> Before (report register) — *As technology advances at high speed, building
> this system is of great importance and provides strong support for the
> development of the field.*
> 原句（报告体）：随着技术的飞速发展，构建这一系统具有十分重要的意义，为行业的
> 发展提供强有力的支撑。

> After (this voice) — *What the system lacks is not scale but a structural
> capability: to turn a risk into a checkable assertion before failure occurs.
> This gap does not close as more resources are added.*
> 改后（本体）：这一系统所缺的不是规模，而是一种结构能力——在失败发生前，把风险
> 变成可核查的断言。这一缺口不会因投入更多资源而自动弥合。

The difference is entirely: filler and mood-adjectives deleted; "important"
replaced by "what is missing and why"; advance by cause, not by praise.
差别全在：删尽套话与情绪形容词；把"重要"换成"缺什么、为何缺"；以因果而非赞颂推进。
